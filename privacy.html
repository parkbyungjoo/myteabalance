Privacy Policy

My Tea Balance Compliance & Data Ethics

Last Updated: January 22, 2026

1. Information We Collect

My Tea Balance is designed as a privacy-first tool. We do not require a user account or any registration. We collect minimal information limited to:

  • Usage Data: Through Google Analytics, we collect anonymous data such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited to help us understand how the service is used.
  • Local Calculations: All birth data and energy profile calculations are processed locally in your browser and are not transmitted to or stored on our servers.

2. Google AdSense & Cookies

We use Google AdSense to serve ads. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this and other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting the Google Ads Settings.

3. Data Integrity & Wellness Purpose

Our tool provides personalized recommendations based on traditional Oriental wisdom (Myungrihak). We do not store your personal life events or specific birth profiles. This tool is for educational and wellness purposes only.

4. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact Dr. Byungjoo Park at contact@myteabalance.com.
